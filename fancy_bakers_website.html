Our Story

A name Jalandhar has trusted since the 1960s.

What started as a small neighbourhood bakery is now a legacy passed through three generations — the same recipes, the same desi ghee, the same love. Fancy Bakers isn't just a bakery. It's a memory in every home it's touched.

1960s

The beginning

Founded in Jalandhar with a simple belief: real ingredients, real craft, no shortcuts.

1980s

A neighbourhood institution

Generations of Jalandhar families made Fancy Bakers their go-to for every celebration.

2000s

Expanding traditions

New products, same values. Eggless baking became our signature across the full range.

Now

Shipping Jalandhar's taste, everywhere

From Canada to the UAE — NRI families now send Fancy Bakers to their loved ones back home.
